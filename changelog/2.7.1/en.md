## Fixes
- Editing a configuration file no longer collapses your YAML onto a single line — multi-line formatting is preserved.
- The change indicator on the gear icon now also lights up when you edit a mounted file, not only the main configuration.
- Context menus no longer wrap their items onto several lines near the right edge of the window.
- After a desktop auto-update, your theme and language are restored reliably instead of briefly resetting to the system default.
