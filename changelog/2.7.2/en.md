## Fixes
- The configuration editor's change markers now work for fields nested inside lists (such as probe settings), and unchanged default values are no longer flagged as added.
- The snapshots dialog now explains why the "Create" and "Import" buttons are disabled (the namespace must be stopped first).
- "Open namespace directory" now opens the namespace folder itself instead of its internal runtime-files subfolder.
- On Windows, the desktop app now starts correctly on a fresh installation.
